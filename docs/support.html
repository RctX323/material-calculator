Common Questions

How do I subscribe?
Open the App and choose a plan on the subscription screen. Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Bid Pro, and make changes there. You can also tap "Manage Subscription" inside the App.

I subscribed but the App is still locked.
Open the App and tap "Restore Purchases" on the subscription screen. If it's still locked, email us at support@bidproapp.ca.

Are the truck capacities accurate?
Standard truck types use Canadian legal payload weights. You can also select "Personal Truck" and enter your own truck's capacity for exact results.
